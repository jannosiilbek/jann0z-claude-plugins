## 1. Introducing EventStorming — Alberto Brandolini  (CANONICAL primary text)

- **Full title:** Introducing EventStorming — An act of Deliberate Collective Learning
- **Author:** Alberto Brandolini
- **Publisher:** Leanpub (self-published, perpetual draft)
- **Canonical URL:** https://leanpub.com/introducing_eventstorming
- **Publisher overview page:** https://www.eventstorming.com/book/
- **Edition / version:** Perpetual draft — no fixed edition number. Continuously updated.
- **Completeness status (as of 2026-06-04):** INCOMPLETE by design. Big Picture mechanics
  are substantially covered; Process Design and Software Design chapters are far from
  complete; the text still contains acknowledged FIXMEs. Self-described as "probably the
  most successful unfinished book on the Leanpub platform." A paper edition is described
  as forthcoming.
- **Class:** cite-only (commercial; do NOT store full text).
- **Why admitted as canonical authority:** It is the inventor's own definitive text and the
  recognized canonical reference for the formalism, even though perpetually unfinished. Its
  completed Big-Picture material is authoritative.

## 2. The EventStorming Handbook — Paul Rayner  (secondary, facilitation-focused)

- **Full title:** The EventStorming Handbook — Unlocking Creativity, Collaboration, and
  Communication for Your Teams
- **Author:** Paul Rayner
- **Publisher:** Leanpub (self-published)
- **Canonical URL:** https://leanpub.com/eventstorming_handbook
- **Edition / version:** Leanpub digital edition (PDF/EPUB/Kindle); no traditional ISBN.
  In circulation since ~2019; updated on Leanpub.
- **Class:** cite-only (commercial; do NOT store full text).
- **Status:** Practitioner/facilitation handbook. Secondary to Brandolini on the formalism
  itself; useful for facilitation mechanics. Listed on the official eventstorming.com
  resources page as a recommended book.
- **Role for this skill:** SECONDARY. Authority on the *formalism / grammar* remains
  Brandolini + the ddd-crew glossary. Cite Rayner only for facilitation framing if needed.
