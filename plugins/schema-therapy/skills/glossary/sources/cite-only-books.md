# Cite-only commercial books (bibliographic identity + public excerpt pointers)

These are copyrighted commercial books. Their full text is **not** gathered. This
file records bibliographic identity, edition, and pointers to authoritative public
excerpts/summaries that may be quoted.

---

## Evans — *Domain-Driven Design: Tackling Complexity in the Heart of Software*

- Author: Eric Evans
- Publisher: Addison-Wesley Professional (Pearson Education)
- First edition: 2004 ("The Big Blue Book"). Still the canonical edition; no second
  edition exists as of 2026-06-04.
- ISBN-13: 978-0321125217
- Status: in print, current. This is the **authoritative source** for the Ubiquitous
  Language concept; every other source here derives from it.
- Public, openly-licensed derivative of its pattern summaries: the **DDD Reference
  (2015-03, CC BY 4.0)**, gathered in this folder as
  `evans-ddd-reference-2015-03.txt` / `.pdf`. The Reference's pattern summaries were
  "extracted from the original text of the book, edited for brevity, but otherwise
  unmodified" and placed in the Creative Commons in 2006 with Pearson's agreement.
  => For the glossary skill, quote the **DDD Reference** (open) rather than the book.
- Relevant chapters in the book (cite-only, for pointers): Ch. 2 "Communication and
  the Use of Language" (Ubiquitous Language); Ch. 14 "Maintaining Model Integrity"
  (Bounded Context).

## Vernon — *Implementing Domain-Driven Design*

- Author: Vaughn Vernon
- Publisher: Addison-Wesley Professional
- Edition: 1st, 2013 ("The Big Red Book"). Current; no later edition.
- ISBN-13: 978-0321834577
- Status: in print, current.
- Relevance: Ch. 1 ("Getting Started with DDD") gives practical guidance on building a
  Ubiquitous Language, including the technique of writing a **glossary of terms with
  simple definitions** and listing alternative/rejected terms (the "forbidden synonym"
  discipline) — directly relevant to this skill. Cite-only; no open full text.
- Vernon's *Domain-Driven Design Distilled* (2016, ISBN 978-0134434421) is a shorter
  companion covering the same Ubiquitous Language guidance; also cite-only.

Note: a free InformIT excerpt of Vernon's "How To Do DDD" chapter material exists at
<https://www.informit.com/articles/article.aspx?p=1944876> (publisher-hosted; quotable
pointer, not gathered as full text).
