# Product — ShiftLoop

## Value proposition

ShiftLoop lets a small hospitality business build a weekly Schedule, lets Employees view
their Shifts and request Swaps, and shows Owners Labor cost against budget as they schedule.

## Target segment

- **Buyer:** owner of a single café, restaurant, or bar with 5–40 hourly Employees.
- **User:** the manager who builds the weekly schedule and the employees who work shifts.

## Problem

Small hospitality schedules are built in spreadsheets and group chats, so shift swaps get
lost and owners only learn they overspent on labor after payroll runs.

|------|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| Starter | 29 USD / month | up to 1 Location, up to 15 Employees | scheduling, swaps, time-off |
| Pro | 79 USD / month | up to 3 Locations, up to 60 Employees | adds labor budget tracking and CSV export |

Trial: 14-day free trial with full Pro features; at expiry the Business becomes read-only
until a Plan is purchased.

## MVP scope

**In scope** (each maps to a capability in `capability-map.md`):
- identity and access
- business and location setup
- team and positions
- availability and time-off
- scheduling
- schedule publishing and notifications
- shift swaps
- labor cost and budget
- subscription and billing

**Out of scope** (do not build, do not spec):
- time clock / clock-in / actual hours worked (labor cost uses scheduled hours)
- payroll export and tax handling
- POS or sales-data integration (no labor-as-percent-of-revenue)
- native mobile apps (responsive web only)
- auto-scheduling or shift-bidding marketplaces
- multi-currency (one currency per Business)

## Success metric

Median time for a manager to publish next week's schedule is under 15 minutes.
